I am a PhD student under advisor [Aslan Askarov](https://askarov.net) in the
[Logic and Semantics group](http://cs.au.dk/research/logic-and-semantics/people-and-visitors/)
at the [Department of Computer Science](https://cs.au.dk) at
[Aarhus University](https://au.dk).

I am studying programming languages and language-based security with a particular focus on language-based methods for online security.
