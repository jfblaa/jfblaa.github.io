Jeppe Fredsgaard Blaabjerg

I am a recent PhD from the Logic and Semantics group at the Department of Computer Science at Aarhus University, where I was advised by Aslan Askarov. I now work as a Program Analysis Engineer at Coana.

I am interested in programming languages (semantics, design, implementation, type systems, program analysis) and computer security (information-flow, language-based security, online security, covert channels).

Additionally, I am fond of functional programming, low-level programming, and compilers.
