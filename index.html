Jeppe Fredsgaard Blaabjerg

I am a PhD student under advisor Aslan Askarov in the Logic and Semantics group at the Department of Computer Science at Aarhus University.

I research languaged-based methods for online security and work with programming languages (semantics, design, type systems, program analysis) and computer security (information-flow, language-based security, online security, covert channels).

I am additionally interested in functional programming, low-level programming, and compilers.
